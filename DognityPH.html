Why We Care

About Us

Our organization encourages volunteers to join our advocacy to protect dogs and break the norm of calling Asong Pinoy (aspin) an askal (Asong Kalye). With the support of thousands of people and their donations, Dognity has spread its help across the islands of the Philippines and has rescued street dogs.

Misson

To protect the welfare of all dogs and promote humane treatment towards them regardless of their breeds.

Visson

To alleviate the stigma against different dog breeds, lessen the number of discrimination against strays, abused, or unadopted dogs.

ADVOCACY

We Value Equality

Promote Kapon

Neutering dogs as young as six to nine months old will have a benefit to the dog and the owner.

Social Media

Spreading awareness in social outlets may convince dog enthusiasts to rescue stray dogs and break the stereotypes that was faced by innocent dogs. The “Dognity Organization” page is already spread in the Philippines with thousands of followers.

Poster

Promote that "Asong Kalye" or askal is already unknown and now it's "Asong Pinoy" or aspin. Small change in name can have a big impact on its welfare, “Mahalin ang sariling atin”.

Encourage private establishments to protect the welfare of Dogs

Treating them like one of the employments is one of the options to treat the strays in a humane way which is already seen in several establishments in the Philippines. Providing them everyday food and shelter by fostering them will help the stray dogs to have a healthy family life.
